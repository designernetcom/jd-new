Joseph Dsouza

The Visionary Behind JD Foundation

Joseph Dsouza, the visionary behind JD Foundation, is a third-generation Konkani Maharashtrian deeply committed to social change. With 36 years of dedicated service as a primary member of the Congress Party and 5 years as an MPCC Regional Representative, he brings invaluable experience in leadership and public service.

Beyond politics, Joseph is a passionate social worker, agriculturist, and businessman. His multifaceted background enables him to connect with diverse communities and address critical issues ranging from rural development to environmental sustainability.

Our Impact
Timeline

A Journey of Dedication and Leadership

1982 Onwards

Active Member of the Congress Committee

Joseph Dsouza began his political journey with the Congress Committee, laying the foundation for decades of dedicated service to the community and the nation. His commitment to grassroots politics has been unwavering ever since.

1988 - 1993

President, Youth Congress, Ward No. 82, Pune

As Youth Congress President, Joseph empowered young voices, fostering leadership and driving youth-focused initiatives in Ward No. 82, Pune.

1993 - 2003

Vice President, Yerawada Bloc Congress, Pune

Joseph’s leadership at Yerawada Bloc strengthened community outreach and promoted inclusive development, focusing on social welfare and local governance.

2004 - 2006

Vice President, Pune City Youth Congress

During his tenure, he worked to mobilize youth participation in civic activities, encouraging the next generation to engage in political and social reform.

2007 - 2010

Secretary, Pune City & District Congress

As Secretary, Joseph played a pivotal role in organizational planning and strategy, ensuring that the Congress Party’s mission and values reached every corner of Pune.

2010 - 2015

Regional Representative, Maharashtra Pradesh Congress Committee

Serving as MPCC Regional Representative, Joseph represented regional interests at the state level, advocating for policies that uplift communities and strengthen Maharashtra’s future.
